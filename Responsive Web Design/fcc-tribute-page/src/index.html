Lapu-Lapu

The First Hero of the Philippines

Lapulapu or Lapu-lapu was a datu of Mactan in Visayas. Modern Philippine society regards him as the first Filipino hero because he was the first native to resist imperial Spanish colonization. He is best known for the Battle of Mactan that happened at dawn on April 27, 1521, where he and his warriors defeated Portuguese explorer Ferdinand Magellan, who was killed in battle. Monuments of Lapulapu have been built in Cebu and Manila, while the Philippine Nation Police and the Bureau of Fire Protection use his image as part of their official seals.
